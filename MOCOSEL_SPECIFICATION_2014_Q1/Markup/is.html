TOPIC
    Is.

TYPE
    <Node>.

CATEGORY
    <Compact>.

DEPENDENCIES
    <Value>.

USAGE
    1) is <Value>, <Value>+,
    2) is < <Value>, <Value>+,
    3) is > <Value>, <Value>+,
    4) is not <Value>, <Value>+.

REMARKS
    Is shall return a positive <Boolean> on success or negative otherwise. Type
    safety shall be guaranteed, excepting for numeric values.

    1) Shall operate on any values and succeed if the first <Value> is
    identical to the rest,
    2) shall operate on numeric values and succeed if the first <Value> is the
    smallest of the rest, 
    3) shall operate on numeric values and succeed if the first <Value> is the
    largest of the rest,
    4) shall operate on any values and suceed if the first <Value> is different
    to the rest.
