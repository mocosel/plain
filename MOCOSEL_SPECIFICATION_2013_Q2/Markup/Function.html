USAGE
    <Statement> -> function [with <Value>*].

REMARKS
    <Statement> as defined in ./Statement.

    <Value> shall be <String> or <Keyword>. Function shall not be limited to
    any number of argument declarations. Arguments shall be immutable, declared
    in function scope. `...` shall be reserved and defined as ordered <Array>
    of unnamed (indexed) arguments.

    <Statement> shall be passed to function as <Target> to list.
