TOPIC
    Node.

TYPE
    <Value>.

CATEGORY
    <Compact>.

DEPENDENCIES
    <Statement>,
    <Keyword>,
    <Value>.

USAGE
    1) [[<Statement>]],
    2) <Keyword>.

REMARKS
    <Statement> as defined in ./Statement. Nodes shall be passed by arguments.

    1) <Statement> must be <Declaration> or <Definition>. <Statement> shall be
    expanded to returning <Value>, plus arguments.
    2) Translation can be supressed. <Keyword> shall be expanded to any
    returning <Value> but <Function>.
