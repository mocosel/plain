TOPIC
    List.

TYPE
    <Value>.

CATEGORY
    <Compact>.

DEPENDENCIES
    <Statement>.

USAGE
    {<Statement>[; <Statement>]*}.

REMARKS
    <Statement> as defined in ./Statement.

    Statements shall be separated (linked) by semicolons. Lists shall be passed
    by arguments and unlike nodes, lists shall not be expanded. Thus, list
    shall be handled directly by that statement which the list is passed to.
