TYPE

CATEGORY
    <Naked>.

DEPENDENCIES
    <Statement>.

USAGE
    {<Statement>[; <Statement>]*}.

REMARKS
    Statements shall be linked by semicolons. Lists shall be passed by
    arguments and unlike nodes, lists shall not be expanded, i.e., any list
    shall be handled directly by that statement which the list is passed to.
