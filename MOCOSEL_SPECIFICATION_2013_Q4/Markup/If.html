TOPIC
    If.

TYPE
    <Node>.

CATEGORY
    <Compact>.

DEPENDENCIES
    <Value>.

USAGE
    if <Value> then <Value> else <Value>.

REMARKS
    The keywords then and else shall not be omitted.

    The conditional statement if shall succeed or fail. In case of failure,
    <Value> on the right side of the statement shall be returned, <Value> on
    the left otherwise.
